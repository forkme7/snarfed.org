[Brid.gy](https://www.brid.gy/) got its start by copying social network comments
to your web site, but it hasn't rested on its laurels! It now also copies
Facebook likes and shares, Twitter retweets, Google+ reshares and +1s, and
Instagram likes.
[Here are](http://aaronparecki.com/notes/2013/11/28/1/github-repo-collections)
[a few](/2013-12-21_myrtle-ladwigs-ambition-suppressant#comments)
[examples](https://www.dentonjacobs.com/2012/08/16/poons-equal-prunes/).

If you're signed up, you've already seen these start to trickle in over the last
week or so. If you're not signed up, feel free to
[try it out](https://www.brid.gy/).

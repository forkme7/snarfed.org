My cousin [Johnny](http://instagram.com/thejohnnysmith) is an artist. He's
worked mostly in film and video, but right now
[his medium of choice is Instagram](http://instagram.com/thejohnnysmith). He
remixes photos into collages that are thoughtful, funny, arresting, beautiful,
sometimes obscene, and always great fun.

His recent series include
[#doppelgangerselfie](http://web.stagram.com/tag/doppelgangerselfie/),
[#artporn](http://web.stagram.com/tag/artporn/),
[#johnnynamedropper](http://web.stagram.com/tag/johnnynamedropper/), and my
favorite so far, [#surrealism](http://web.stagram.com/tag/surrealism/). Here are
a few highlights.

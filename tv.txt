Television shows I
[want to watch](#to_watch) and
[have watched](#watched).
<good> I liked,
<ok> were just ok,
<bad> I didn't like.
Also see [other lists](/lists).

Naturally, this isn't comprehensive - I've watched plenty of shows that aren't
on this list - and it's biased toward my taste. If I mark a show
<bad>, I may still think it's good overall, but just doesn't match my
own personal taste, and vice versa. I tend to prefer scripted comedies and
serial dramas.

Also, I don't actually have a TV, much less cable or satellite, so I watch on
sites like [Hulu](http://www.hulu.com/) and [Netflix](http://www.netflix.com/)
instead.

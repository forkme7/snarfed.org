![good](/good.png) I liked,
![ok](/ok.png) were just ok,
![bad](/bad.png) I didn't like.

Naturally, this isn't comprehensive -
I've tried others that aren't listed here -
and it's biased toward my taste. I tend to prefer darker beers: stouts,
porters, and nut brown and red ales. I don't mind lighter ales and lagers, but
some, like lambics and Belgian ales, I'm not a fan of.

snarfed.org PyBlosxom flavour

[![snarfed_flavour_screenshot_thumb.jpg](/space/snarfed_flavour_screenshot_thumb.jpg)](snarfed_flavour_screenshot.jpg)

[PyBlosxom](http://snipsnap.org/), the server that runs this site,
supports
[flavours](http://pyblosxom.sourceforge.net/blog/registry/flavours?sortby=path).
They change your PyBlosxom site's appearance, using
[templates](file:///home/ryanb/shared/docs/pyblosxom/manual/c224.html#AEN235),
[CSS](http://www.w3.org/Style/CSS/), images, and other files, without changing
its content. There's a [longer
description](http://pyblosxom.sf.net/1.3.1/manual/c224.html) on the
[PyBlosxom site](http://pyblosxom.sf.net/).

The snarfed.org flavour is [available for download](/space/snarfed_flavour.tar.gz).
To try it out on your site,
[download snarfed_flavour.tar.gz](/space/snarfed_flavour.tar.gz) and copy the
contents of `datadir/` and `flavourdir/snarfed.flav` into your PyBlosxom's
datadir and flavourdir, respectively. Then, edit `config.py` and add this line:

    py['default_flavour'] = 'snarfed'

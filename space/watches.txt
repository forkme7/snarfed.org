watch shopping

(thinking out loud here...)

two watches, both similar. mechanical automatic would be nice, but i'll consider
quartz. manual would be doable, but annoying.

circular cases. analog, standard display. no chronograph or extra complications,
ideally not even day of month, but that one's negotiable.

actually, i kind of like small seconds. i think i'd prefer that to day of the
month. it's less common though. otherwise, don't care if there's a second hand.

thin would be nice, but not necessary. window on the back of the case to show
the mechanism would be nice, but not necessary.

- one yellow gold, dark brown leather band, probably white face
- one platinum or white gold or steel, bracelet, either hidden deployant or
  jeweler's clasp, black or white or blue face

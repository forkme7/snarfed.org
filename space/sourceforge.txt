[![/space/sourceforge.png](/space/sourceforge.png)](http://sourceforge.net/people/viewprofile.php?user_id=751286)[SourceForge](http://sf.net/)
is a site that hosts open source projects, similar to
[savannah](/space/savannah). They provide web space, file hosting, CVS, compile
farms, bug and patch trackers, forums, mailing lists, and more.

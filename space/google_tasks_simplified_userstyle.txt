[google_tasks_simplified.css](google_tasks_simplified.css) is a
[userstyle](http://userstyles.org/help/userstyles) that simplifies
[Google Tasks](https://mail.google.com/tasks/desktop) by hiding a few things,
e.g. the left side panel with the task lists, until you hover over them. It also
removes some toolbar buttons and enlarges the notes textarea in the task detail
page.

Google Tasks Simplified also has
[its own page](http://userstyles.org/styles/36659) on
[userstyles.org](http://userstyles.org/).

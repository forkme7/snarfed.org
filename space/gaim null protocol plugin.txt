Nullprpl is a no-op protocol plugin for [gaim](http://gaim.sf.net/). You can
create accounts with it, sign on and off, add buddies, set away messages and
status, and send and receive IMs...all without connecting to a server.

Nullprpl is intended to streamline most common gaim development (other than
protocol plugins, of course). It also allows you to hack on gaim [while you're
disconnected](https://sourceforge.net/mailarchive/message.php?msg_id=9534285).

Nullprpl is currently only supported in Gaim 1.x. I'm working on a big
expansion, including all standard IM functionality and Gaim 2.0 support. I'll
post here when it's ready.

Nullprpl requires [Gaim](http://gaim.sf.net/) 1.x and
[libtool](http://www.gnu.org/software/libtool/libtool.html) 1.4 or higher (on
\*nix) or [Cygwin](http://www.cygwin.com/)/[MinGW](http://mingw.org/) (on
Windows). Windows support was generously contributed by [Andrew
Hart](http://sourceforge.net/users/arhart/). Nullprpl is copyright 2004 [Ryan
Barrett](http://ryan.barrett.name/), and is distributed under the
[GPL](http://www.gnu.org/licenses/gpl.html).

dotfiles
They may not be earth-shattering, but I've posted my dotfiles for programs I use
often, including [Emacs](http://gnu.org/software/emacs/),
[Ion](http://iki.fi/tuomov/ion), [Pine](http://www.washington.edu/pine/),
[tcsh](http://www.tcsh.org/), [screen](http://www.gnu.org/software/screen/),
[Python](http://python.org/), and
[readline](http://cnswww.cns.cwru.edu/php/chet/readline/rltop.html):

[http://ryan.barrett.name/dotfiles/](http://ryan.barrett.name/dotfiles/)

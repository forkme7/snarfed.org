directions to google

Visiting me at Google? I'm in the San Francisco office, in the
[Hills Plaza](http://www.hillsplazasf.com/) building at 345 Spear St. Use the
map below for directions.

You can take the [Muni](http://sfmuni.org/)
[N Judah](http://transit.511.org/schedules/index.aspx#m1=S&m2=rail&cid=SF&rted=N) or
[K/T Ingleside](http://transit.511.org/schedules/index.aspx#m1=S&m2=rail&routeid=26606&cid=SF)
to the Folsom stop, or take
[Bart](http://www.bart.gov/) to the
[Embarcadero](http://www.bart.gov/stations/embr/) stop and walk down Spear a
few blocks. If you're driving, there's metered parking on the street and
a parking garage in the building.

Take the elevator to the fourth floor. If the receptionist is there, you can
just ask for me. Otherwise, call me at 650-253-5568. Use the touch screen to
print yourself a guest badge and sign over the NDA, your bank account, and
your firstborn child.

Feel free to call me if you get lost. See you soon!

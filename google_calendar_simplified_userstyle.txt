[google_calendar_simplified.css](https://github.com/snarfed/misc/blob/master/userscripts/google_calendar_simplified.css) is a
[userstyle](http://userstyles.org/help/userstyles) that simplifies
[Google Calendar](https://calendar.google.com/) by removing the calendar header
(except for the Settings link), footer, and other things. It was inspired by
[Aziz](http://userstyles.org/users/8006)'s
[original (deprecated) userstyle](http://userstyles.org/styles/20945).

Google Calendar Simplified also has
[its own page](http://userstyles.org/styles/36781) on
[userstyles.org](http://userstyles.org/).

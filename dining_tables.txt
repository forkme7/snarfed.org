dining room tables

I've been shopping for a dining room table. I'm leaning toward a single or
joined slab of black or claro walnut, with a
[live edge](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Live_edge)
and a solid iron, steel, or bronze pedestal base.

Here are some of the candidate
[designers and tables](#designers),
[lumber providers](#lumber), and
[metal workers](#metalworkers). See
[the post](/2010-04-12_dining_table_shopping) for more details.

directions to google

Visiting me at Google? I work at both the [San Francisco](#sfo) and
[Mountain View](#mtv) offices. Maps and directions below. When you arrive, ask
the receptionist for me, print yourself a guest badge, and sign over the NDA,
your bank account, and your firstborn child. See you soon!


<a name="sfo" />
The San Francisco office is at
[345 Spear St. at Folsom](http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=345+Spear+St,+94105+(Google+San+Francisco)&amp;sll=37.789904,-122.390576&amp;sspn=0.00669,0.009656&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;ll=37.801036,-122.386065&amp;spn=0.023737,0.036478&amp;z=14&amp;iwloc=addr&amp;source=embed),
on the 4th floor. You can take the [Muni](http://sfmuni.org/)
[N Judah](http://transit.511.org/schedules/index.aspx#m1=S&m2=rail&cid=SF&rted=N)
or
[K/T Ingleside](http://transit.511.org/schedules/index.aspx#m1=S&m2=rail&routeid=26606&cid=SF)
to the Folsom stop, or take [Bart](http://www.bart.gov/) to the
[Embarcadero](http://www.bart.gov/stations/embr/) stop and walk down Spear a few
blocks. If you're driving, there's metered parking on the street and a
parking garage in the building.

<a name="mtv" />
The Mountain View office is at
[1600 Amphitheatre Pkwy](http://maps.google.com/maps?f=q&amp;source=embed&amp;hl=en&amp;geocode=&amp;q=google+mountain+view+%22bldg+43%22&amp;aq=&amp;sll=37.421768,-122.084327&amp;sspn=0.002573,0.004656&amp;ie=UTF8&amp;hq=&amp;hnear=Google+Bldg+43,+Google+Dr,+Mountain+View,+Santa+Clara,+California+94043&amp;t=m&amp;ll=37.42157,-122.084008&amp;spn=0.010293,0.018625&amp;z=14&amp;iwloc=A).
I'm in building 43. There's a
[shuttle](http://www.caltrain.com/schedules/Shuttles/Shoreline_Shuttle.html) from
[Caltrain](http://www.caltrain.com/)'s
[Mountain View station](http://www.caltrain.com/stations/mountainviewstation.html)
that runs during commute hours, but otherwise you'll need to drive or take a cab.

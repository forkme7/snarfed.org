[Savannah](http://savannah.nongnu.org/) is a site that hosts GNU-related open source projects, similar to [SourceForge](/SourceForge). They provide web space, file hosting, CVS, compile farms, bug and patch trackers, forums, mailing lists, and more. Savannah focuses on [free software](http://fsf.org/) related to the [GNU project](http://gnu.org/).

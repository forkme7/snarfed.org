Life lessons

* You can't change other people. You can only change yourself.
* Care less what other people think.
* People are different. More than you think.
* [Tell yourself a different story.](http://tinybuddha.com/blog/change-life-changing-stories-tell-yourself/)
* Empathize. Care about other people as much as yourself, or more.
* First, find common goals.
* Create.
* All things in moderation, including moderation.
* Experience great art regularly. All different kinds.
* Self care is important. Build healthy habits: food, sleep, exercise, stress, relationships.
* Actions speak louder than words.
